About Us

Hello, my name is Kate and I am a party decorator based out of Ocean County, NJ. I work with my clients to make their events memorable and create balloon displays to make their special day picture perfect.

I started in the business as an event planner much throughout my youth and always enjoyed helping people bring their designs to life. I started my company "A Balloon Happenin" as my own venture and fulfill my dreams as a designer. During my time event planning I adored the memories that we could help create and wanted to take my skills on the road. I started my company in 2021 and will never look back. My goal is to help my customers bring their party to life with balloon designs of their choosing. I have in the past built displays of peoples favorite Disney characters, favorite shows, or even custom designs chosen by our more creatives minded customers. Check our Gallery to see what we've done!
